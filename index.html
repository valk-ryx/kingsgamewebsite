King's Game Group

Bringing community to the roads

About Us

Here at King's Game, we offer high quality ready to use products, frequent community events and a professional game with a realistic setting that bridges the gap between physical shopping centers and the modern virtual experience within our new Kingstreet Plaza, coming later this year.

Our Community

Here at King's Game we have an active community where you can chat with others and make friends as well as taking part in frequent events, with our goal being to always give our community something to do, whether that simply be chatting via text or playing in one of our events.

Our Products

We only sell products of the highest quality, with all of our products being EAN ready, allowing you to simply insert them into whatever Ro-Store you'd like, no questions asked! Along with the quality of our products, we frequently release new products for all Ro-Stores to use.

Our Goal

Our goal on the Roblox platform is to always maintain a high standard, providing the Roblox community with a way to showcase their creations in our upcoming version 3 game which will allow people to showcase and view the finest picks of our communities work, allowing users on the Roblox platform to expand their businesses easily and to allow people to find new experiences that they may enjoy.

Our Permit Holders

We also have an option to become a permit holder, this gives you access to exclusive perks and helps to support further development of King's Game, allowing us to expand and allow more people to benifit from our games. You are able to become a permit holder via our Roblox gamepass which allows you to have a rainbow name in-game and more!

Our Game

Here at King's Game, we offer a Roblox game that has a realistic setting and allows people to connect whilst discovering new experiences on the Roblox platform. In our new Version 3 game, Kingstreet Plaza, we will bridge the gap between physical shopping centres and the virtual world of Roblox, allowing people to explore different stores on the Roblox platform within one game as well as engage in frequent community events through our Discord server.

Our Discord Server

As well as our Roblox game and community, we also have an active server on Discord where our community can connect through more than a simple in-game text chat, our community is able to connect through frequent events, voice chats and more. Our Discord server allows people to make friends forever rather than just the one time meet up you might get from Roblox.

Products

Here at King's Game, we not only provide a game for you to explore other user's creations, we also provide resources for you to build your own experiences on the Roblox platform with frequent Ro-Store product releases, made with the community in mind.
